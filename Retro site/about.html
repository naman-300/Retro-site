Our History

Sony Corporation, a Japanese multinational conglomerate, was founded by Masaru Ibuka and Akio Morita in 1946. The company's journey into the world of music began in 1979 with the introduction of the iconic Sony Walkman.

Since then, Sony has been a pioneer in the field of consumer electronics, including audio technology, video, gaming, and more. The Walkman, with its innovative design and portability, revolutionized the way people experienced music on-the-go.
